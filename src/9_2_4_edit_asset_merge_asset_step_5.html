What will be copied?

All recorded events from the duplicate asset will be copied over to the "winner".

What will be deleted?

The duplicate asset will be deleted including all of its attributes.

Can this operation be undone?

No - this is a final operation so please ensure you have selected the correct duplicate.

When will the asset be merged?

Once you confirm the merge the process of moving the events will start. You will receive an email confirmation once its completed.
