View Customer - MErge

1. UNIROPE LTD (this record will) be the "loser" of this merge

2. Select the customer you want to keep ("the winner")

This is the customer that wil still exist - the "winner" of the merge. Search for the customer you want to keep:

Name

3. Confirm Merge

What will be copied?

All assets, events, schedules, divisions and users from duplicate owner will be copied over to the "winner".

What will be deleted?

The duplicate owner will be archived which you can view any time by clicking the View Archived tab.

Can this operation be undone?

No - this is a final operation so please ensure you have selected the correct duplicate.

When will the two records be merged?

Once you confirm the merge the process of moving the data will start. You will receive an email confirmation once it''s completed.
